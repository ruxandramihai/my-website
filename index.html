Research Project at Machine Intelligence Lab

UK Dementia Research Institute | February 2023 - September 2023

During my time at the UK Dementia Research Institute, supervised by Prof. Payam Barnaghi, I completed my master’s thesis on generative models for clinical and EEG data. I trained and evaluated a denoising diffusion probabilistic model for EEG data synthesis and implemented deep learning models for EEG classification in patients with epilepsy. I analyzed the weight correlations in the generative model to investigate patterns in EEG signals between seizure and non-seizure groups.
